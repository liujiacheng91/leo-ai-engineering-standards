# Mapping Rules Skill

Make rules explicit before implementation.

Rules:

1. Require source field, target field, rule, example, validation samples and business confirmation.
2. Do not implement before rules are confirmed.
3. Output `Mapping_Rules.md` or `Business_Rules.md` under the standard case folder.
