# Msg310X12（X12 4010 / 310 Freight Receipt and Invoice (Ocean)）结构说明

## 1. 概述

Msg310X12 是一个 X12 信封级容器，承载：

- ISA/GS：交换/功能组头
- MSG310（交易集 310）：可重复出现
- GE/IEA：功能组/交换尾

模型与输出顺序由以下类实现：

- [Msg310X12.java](file:///d:/SourceCode/arch/edi/edi-business-strategy-realization-develop-v1.0.1/edi-standard-define/edi-x12/src/main/java/com/pobing/edi/platform/x12/v4010/MSG310/Msg310X12.java)
- [Msg310.java](file:///d:/SourceCode/arch/edi/edi-business-strategy-realization-develop-v1.0.1/edi-standard-define/edi-x12/src/main/java/com/pobing/edi/platform/x12/v4010/MSG310/Msg310.java)

对应 EDI 映射（段出现次数/层级）：

- [edimappingconfig.xml](file:///d:/SourceCode/arch/edi/edi-business-strategy-realization-develop-v1.0.1/edi-standard-define/edi-x12/src/main/resources/x12/v4010/msg310/edimappingconfig.xml)
